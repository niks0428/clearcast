Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the personal tool operated by Vlad (the "App") used to manage content on Pinterest and other platforms. This App is for personal use only and is not distributed to third parties.

1. Data We Collect

This App does not collect personal data from any users. It operates solely on behalf of the account owner to create and manage Pinterest pins and boards linked to the owner's own online store.

2. How We Use Data

The App uses the Pinterest API only to:

  • Create and manage boards on the owner's Pinterest Business account
  • Create pins linking to the owner's digital product listings

No data is stored, sold, or shared with any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

This App connects to the Pinterest API in accordance with Pinterest's Privacy Policy and Developer Terms of Service.

4. Data Retention

No personal data is retained by this App. OAuth tokens used to authenticate with Pinterest are stored locally on the owner's private server and are not accessible to any third party.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact: vlad@clearcast.site
